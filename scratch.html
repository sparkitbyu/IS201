Why do I love Soccer?


  1. The Competition
    • As a competitive person, I love being able to fight, excel at, and win, and soccer is a fun way to do that.
    • Also, weekly practices with the right team can be super competitive, which makes playing with the girls so fun.
    • Lastly, I LOVE game days. They are my favorite day of the week... it is the best feeling.
  2. Keeps me Fit
    • Soccer keeps me physically fit through hard work in practices and games.
    • It also keeps me mentally fit, by building my drive and learning to overcome challenges.
  3. The Family
    • Most of all, I love the girls. I love being part of a team.
    • Also, I love the network it gives me, it lets me meet people I never would have!
    • Plus, I get to learn from and make connections with different coaches around my community.
